# ITTN-folio table of concordance

A chapter of an income tax folio is usually an updated version of the technical content in one or more income tax interpretation bulletins (IT bulletin). The folios might also incorporate related material contained in an income tax technical news publication (ITTN). Chapters will be published on an as-completed basis. At that time, any IT bulletin or ITTNupdated by a folio chapter will be cancelled. It is expected that the update process will take several years.
