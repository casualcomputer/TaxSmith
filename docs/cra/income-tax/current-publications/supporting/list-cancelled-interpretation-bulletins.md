# List of cancelled interpretation bulletins

As part of a Canada Revenue Agency initiative to remove outdated and redundant content from its website, all previously archived income tax interpretation bulletins (IT bulletins) as well as nine current IT bulletins were cancelled and removed on September 30, 2012.

The table below lists the IT bulletins that have been cancelled. The table is divided into three parts:

[Part 1](#Pt-one): Previously archived IT bulletins cancelled on September 30, 2012

[Part 2](#Pt-two): Active IT bulletins cancelled on September 30, 2012

[Part 3](#Pt-three): IT bulletins cancelled for other reasons

**Note:**

IT bulletins replaced by an income tax folio are listed in the [Table of concordance between IT bulletins and income tax folios](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/income-tax/table-concordance-bulletin-folio.html).
