# What's new in income tax folios

Income tax folios are a series of technical publications that reflect the Canada Revenue Agency's interpretation of federal income tax provisions as they relate to various topics. They were introduced in March 2013 and are gradually replacing income tax interpretation bulletins. To see the complete list of folios, visit the [Income tax folio index](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/income-tax/income-tax-folios-index.html) page.
