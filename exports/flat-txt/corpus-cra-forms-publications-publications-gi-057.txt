# GI-057 CANCELLED Ontario and British Columbia: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax-Memberships

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST applies to sales of memberships and lifetime memberships where the membership period includes the July 1, 2010 implementation date of the HST in Ontario and British Columbia.
