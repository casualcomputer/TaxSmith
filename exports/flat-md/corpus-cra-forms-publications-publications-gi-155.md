# GI-155 CANCELLED British Columbia: Transition to the Goods and Services Tax - Payment of the GST/HST by B.C. Government Entities

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains when the GST or the HST applies to taxable supplies of property and services acquired by B.C. government entities during the period that includes April 1, 2013.
