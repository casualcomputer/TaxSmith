# EDM1-1-5 CANCELLED Instrument Approval

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2026-04-15 and replaced with [EDM1-1-5 Instrument approval](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/excise-duty/excise-duty-memoranda/edm1-1-5-instrument-approval.html).

This memorandum lists various specifications and explains the procedures for the examination or re-examination of instruments that are used to measure the volume and absolute ethyl alcohol content (strength) of alcohol for purposes of the Excise Act, 2001 (the ''Act'').
