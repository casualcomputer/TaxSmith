# GI-041 CANCELLED The 2008 GST/HST Rate Reduction and Streamlined Methods of Accounting for Small Businesses

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains how the proposed reduction in the rate of the GST and the federal component of the HST will apply to the streamlined methods of accounting for transactions that straddle January 1, 2008, the date the reduced rates come into effect.
