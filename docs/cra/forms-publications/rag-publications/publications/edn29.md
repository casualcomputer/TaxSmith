# EDN29 CANCELLED Tobacco Stamping Regime - Excise Stamp Order Process

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2026-04-15 and replaced with [EDN29 Tobacco stamping regime – Excise stamp order process](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/excise-duty/excise-duty-notices/edn29-tobacco-stamping-regime-excise-stamp-order-process.html).

The purpose of this notice is to provide guidance to tobacco licensees and prescribed persons seeking to acquire excise stamps under the new tobacco stamping regime in accordance with the Excise Act, 2001 (Act).
