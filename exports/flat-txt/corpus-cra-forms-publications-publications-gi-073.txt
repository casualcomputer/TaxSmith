# GI-073 CANCELLED Ontario and British Columbia: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Payment of the GST/HST by Ontario and B.C. Government Entities

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST applies to supplies of property and services to Ontario and British Columbia government entities during the period that includes the July 1, 2010 implementation date in Ontario and British Columbia.
