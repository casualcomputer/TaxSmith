REASONS FOR ORDER
Beaubier, D.J.
[1] Upon the appearance before the Court of Gregory Perlinski, counsel for the Respondent; and upon the agent for the Appellant, Mr. Dale Somerville, C.A. appearing before the Court and advising the Court that the Appellant has instructed Mr. William Mouris, Barrister & Solicitor, of 201 – 4104 A – 97th Street, Edmonton, Alberta, T6E 5Y6, telephone (780) 414-6080, to act as counsel for the Appellant and Mr. Mouris having entered an appearance, the Court heard from Mr. Perlinski and Mr. Mouris. It is therefore ordered that the address for service of the Appellant is Mr. William Mouris, Barrister & Solicitor, of 201 – 4104 A – 97th Street, Edmonton, Alberta, T6E 5Y6.
[2] At the resumption of this hearing at 9:15 a.m. on June 5, 2009, the parties filed a discontinuance.
