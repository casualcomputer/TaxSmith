# Change it up: Pay your taxes online

## Transcript
Hi, I'm Brian. I'm a taxpayer just like you.
People tell me that I'm kind of an old school type of guy.
And when it came to paying my taxes, I liked the peace of mind of writing a cheque, tucking it into an envelope, and mailing it off.
But that all changed when I found out I could pay my taxes to the Canada Revenue Agency online! In fact, more and more Canadians are doing it.
So I wondered, what did they know that I didn't?
Well, it turns out that paying your taxes online is faster, easier, and cheaper.
What's not to like about that?
An online payment to the CRA is as simple as making an online payment to your water or hydro provider.
You can pay online through your bank or directly on the CRA website.
Choose the payment option that best suits your needs… with no worries.
Online payments are completely secure and just as official as any paper payment record.
If you pay your income tax in installments, paying online means you can forget about missing deadlines, or being charged for late payments.
You can even set up preauthorized payments on the CRA website.
So, forget about being old school. Save time. Pay online.
**(Go to Canada.ca/payments to get started)**
