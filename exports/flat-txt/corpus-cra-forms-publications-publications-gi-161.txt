# GI-161 CANCELLED Prince Edward Island: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Returns and Exchanges

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST would apply to returns and exchanges of goods made in Prince Edward Island during the period that includes the April 1, 2013 implementation date for the HST in Prince Edward Island.
