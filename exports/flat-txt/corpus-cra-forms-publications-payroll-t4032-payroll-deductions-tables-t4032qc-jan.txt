# T4032QC Payroll Deductions Tables - EI, and income tax deductions - Quebec - Effective January 1, 2026

## Notice to the reader

Publication T4032, *Payroll Deductions Tables*, is available in sections for each province and territory. Section A (available in both HTML and PDF formats) contains general information. Sections B and C (available in PDF format only) contain the payroll deductions tables for Employment Insurance premiums and the payroll deductions tables for federal tax deductions.

Please note that you can also use the [Payroll Deductions Online Calculator (PDOC)](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/digital-services-businesses/payroll-deductions-online-calculator.html) to calculate federal tax deductions and employment insurance premiums.
