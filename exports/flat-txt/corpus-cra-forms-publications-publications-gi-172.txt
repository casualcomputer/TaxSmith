# GI-172 CANCELLED Public Service Bodies' Rebate for Charities Resident Only in British Columbia

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet will help you calculate your public service bodies' rebate (PSB rebate) if you are a charity that is: resident in British Columbia; not resident in any other province; and not a selected public service body (selected PSB).
