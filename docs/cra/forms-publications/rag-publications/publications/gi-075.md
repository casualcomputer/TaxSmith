# GI-075 CANCELLED Ontario and British Columbia: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Returns and Exchanges

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains how the goods and services tax (GST) and the HST applies to returns and exchanges of goods during a period that includes the July 1, 2010 implementation date of the HST in Ontario and British Columbia.
