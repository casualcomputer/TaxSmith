# NOTICE301 CANCELLED Prince Edward Island HST Rate Increase - Questions and Answers on General Transitional Rules for Personal Property and Services

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

On June 16, 2016, the Government of Prince Edward Island released transitional rules to determine the HST rate applicable to transactions that straddle the October 1, 2016, implementation date. This notice provides questions and answers on housing rebates and transitional rules for housing and other real property, which have been enacted in the Regulations Amending Various GST/HST Regulations, No. 9 (Prince Edward Island) (SOR/2016-212).
