AMENDED REASONS FOR JUDGMENT
Hogan J.
I. Introduction
[1] On April 27, 2021, the appellant filed a notice of appeal with the Tax Court of Canada.
[2] The appellant argued that a capital loss of $141,564 resulted from a redemption. This loss was deferred in the late Georges Robillard’s final return.
[3] In the notice of assessment issued on March 30, 2016, the Minister set the amount of this capital loss at $68,442.
[4] At the hearing, the appellant discontinued its appeal on the issue of the $141,564 capital loss having been deferred in the late Georges Robillard’s final return.
II. Conclusion
[5] For the aforementioned reasons, the appellant’s appeal against the assessment for tax year 2012 is dismissed.
