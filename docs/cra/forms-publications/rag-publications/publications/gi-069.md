# GI-069 CANCELLED Ontario and British Columbia: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Direct Sellers and Independent Sales Contractors

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST applies to sales of exclusive products made by direct sellers and independent sales contractors (ISCs) in Ontario and British Columbia during the period that includes July 1, 2010.
