# Current publications

[Income tax folios (Folios)](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/income-tax/income-tax-folios-index.html) A folio gives the CRA's technical interpretation and position on certain provisions contained in the *Income Tax Act*. Income tax folios will replace related income tax interpretation bulletins.

[Income tax information circulars (ICs)](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/current-income-tax-information-circulars-6.html) An IC generally provides information about administrative, enforcement, or procedural matters relating to income tax law, such as collections policies and the voluntary disclosure program.

[Income tax interpretation bulletins (ITs)](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/current-income-tax-interpretation-bulletins-9.html) An IT gives the CRA's technical interpretation and position on certain provisions contained in income tax law. Income tax interpretation bulletins will eventually be replaced by relevant income tax folios.

[Income Tax Technical News (ITTN)](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/income-tax/current-income-tax-technical-news-ittn.html) Issued on an ad hoc basis, an ITTN provides the CRA's technical interpretation of various provisions of income tax law. The newsletter format allows a new or revised interpretation to be released quickly.
