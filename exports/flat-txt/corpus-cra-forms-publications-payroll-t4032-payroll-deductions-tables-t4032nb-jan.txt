# T4032NB Payroll Deductions Tables - CPP, EI, and income tax deductions - New Brunswick - Effective January 1, 2026

## Notice to the reader

Publication T4032, *Payroll Deductions Tables*, is available in sections for each province and territory. Section A (available in both HTML and PDF formats) contains general information. Sections B to E (available in PDF format only) contain the payroll deductions tables for Canada Pension Plan contributions, Employment Insurance premiums, and federal and New Brunswick provincial tax deductions.

Please note that you can also use the [Payroll Deductions Online Calculator (PDOC)](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/digital-services-businesses/payroll-deductions-online-calculator.html) to quickly calculate federal and provincial payroll deductions.
