# EDN28 CANCELLED Becoming a Prescribed Person under the New Tobacco Stamping Regime

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2026-04-15 and replaced with [EDN28 Becoming a prescribed person under the new tobacco stamping regime](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/excise-duty/excise-duty-notices/edn28-becoming-prescribed-person-under-new-tobacco-stamping-regime.html).

The purpose of this notice is to provide guidance on the manner in which to apply to be a prescribed person under the Excise Act, 2001 (Act) for purposes of purchasing, possessing and applying excise stamps on tobacco products.
