# Income tax folio index

## 

Income Tax Folios will be replacing interpretation bulletins over the next several years as the technical content is updated.

For additional information, please refer to [Introducing income tax folios](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/income-tax/introducing-income-tax-folios.html) and the [Interpretation bulletin - income tax folio table of concordance](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/income-tax/table-concordance-bulletin-folio.html). For a list of the most recently added or updated folios, see [What's new in income tax folios](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/income-tax/whats-new-income-tax-folios.html).

## Income Tax Folios are organized into seven Series:

- [Series 1 Individuals](#S1)

- [Series 2 Employers and Employees](#S2)

- [Series 3 Property, Investments and Savings Plans](#S3)

- [Series 4 Businesses](#S4)

- [Series 5 International and Residency](#S5)

- [Series 6 Trusts](#S6)

- [Series 7 Charities, Non-profit Organizations, and other Tax Exempt Entities](#s7)

Each series is comprised of several folios. A folio will contain free-standing chapters, each of which will address specific aspects of the folio topic.
