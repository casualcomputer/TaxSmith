# Air Travellers Security Charge (ATSC) Rates

The air travellers security charge (ATSC) is a security charge paid by air travellers for an air transportation service within and outside the continental zone, under the *Air Travellers Security Charge Act*. The continental zone means Canada, the United States (except Hawaii), and the Islands of St. Pierre and Miquelon.

In this publication, **transborder** refers to a destination in the United States (except Hawaii) or in the Islands of St. Pierre and Miquelon.

In addition, **other international** refers to a destination outside the continental zone.

For general information on the application of the ATSC, go to [Questions and Answers – Air Travellers Security Charge (ATSC)](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/technical-information/individuals-air-travellers-security-charge-atsc/questions-answers-air-travellers-security-charge-atsc.html).

For more information on the application of the GST/HST, refer to [GST/HST Memorandum 28-3, Passenger Transportation Services](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/28-3.html).
