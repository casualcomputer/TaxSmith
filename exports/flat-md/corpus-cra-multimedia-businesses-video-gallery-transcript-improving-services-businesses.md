# Improving services for businesses

## Transcript
As an employer, you are the foundation of Canada’s growing economy.
You do more than just create jobs; you give back to communities and contribute to social programs.
Being an employer also comes with many responsibilities – from withholding and remitting payroll deductions to filing T4 slips on time, so you don’t face penalties and interest.
The Canada Revenue Agency knows a lot of time and effort go into managing payroll. That’s why we’re here to help make meeting your payroll obligations easier
We’ve already decreased the number of times you must remit your source deductions, introducing quarterly remittances for eligible new small employers, and simplified Form PD7A.
And it doesn’t stop there.
We’re constantly improving our online services,
Using “My Business Account”, you can now file returns, view account activities, manage banking information, respond to notices, and more, all with just a few clicks so you can free up your time, and focus on growing your business.
Your time is valuable
The CRA is committed to helping your business prosper. For more payroll information, go to canada.ca/payroll.
