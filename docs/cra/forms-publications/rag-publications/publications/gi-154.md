# GI-154 CANCELLED Prince Edward Island: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Payment of the GST/HST by Prince Edward Island Government Entities

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains when the GST or the HST applies to taxable supplies of property and services made to P.E.I. government entities during the period that includes April 1, 2013.
