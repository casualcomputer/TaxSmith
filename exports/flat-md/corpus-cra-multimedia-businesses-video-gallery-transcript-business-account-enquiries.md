# My Business Account Enquiries

## Transcript
Welcome to My Business Account Enquiries, a brand new service inside inside the Canada Revenue Agency's My Business Account.
Have a tax-related question about your business?
Starting in April 2012, you can ask us online, and we'll answer you online.
Simply log into My Business Account, and click on Enquiries service.
Our response to you will show up in the Message Centre.
It's easy and completely confidential.
So if you haven't signed up for My Business Account, register here: [www.cra.gc.ca/mybusinessaccount](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/digital-services-businesses/business-account.html)
Or if you're an employee or representative of a business, register here: [www.cra.gc.ca/representatives](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/represent-a-client.html)
