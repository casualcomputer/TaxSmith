# GI-076 CANCELLED Ontario and British Columbia: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Continuous Supplies and Budget Payment Arrangements

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST applies to continuous supplies, and supplies made under a budget payment arrangement, during a period that includes the July 1, 2010 implementation date of the HST in Ontario and British Columbia.
