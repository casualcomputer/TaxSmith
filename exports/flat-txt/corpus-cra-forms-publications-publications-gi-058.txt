# GI-058 CANCELLED Ontario and British Columbia: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax-Admissions

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST applies to admissions to events that take place during the period that includes the July 1, 2010 implementation date for the HST in Ontario and British Columbia.
