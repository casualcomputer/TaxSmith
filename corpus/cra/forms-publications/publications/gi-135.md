# GI-135 CANCELLED Prince Edward Island: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Services

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST would apply to taxable supplies of services made in Prince Edward Island where the services are performed during the period that includes the proposed April 1, 2013 implementation date for the HST in Prince Edward Island.
