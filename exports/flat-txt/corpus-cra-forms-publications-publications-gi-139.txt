# GI-139 CANCELLED Prince Edward Island: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Admissions

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST would apply to taxable sales of admissions made in Prince Edward Island to events that take place during the period that includes the proposed April 1, 2013 implementation date for the HST in Prince Edward Island.
