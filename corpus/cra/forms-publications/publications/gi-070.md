# GI-070 CANCELLED Ontario and British Columbia: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Goods

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST applies to the following supplies: sales of goods; sales of a combination of goods and services; sales of periodical publications by subscription; and leases and licences of goods.
