# NOTICE270 CANCELLED Elimination of the HST in British Columbia in 2013 - Questions and Answers

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

Effective April 1, 2013, the 12% HST would no longer apply on taxable supplies of personal property or services made in British Columbia or to personal property or services imported or brought into British Columbia. The following detailed set of questions and answers is provided to assist businesses and consumers in understanding how the transition from HST to GST would apply.
