# GI-160 CANCELLED Prince Edward Island: Transition to the Harmonized Sales Tax - Goods

## This publication was cancelled

This publication was cancelled as of 2025-11-12.

This info sheet explains whether the goods and services tax (GST) or the HST would apply to the following taxable supplies made in Prince Edward Island during the period that includes the proposed April 1, 2013, implementation date for the HST in Prince Edward Island: sales of goods; sales of a cobination of goods and services, sales of subscriptions to periodical publications; and leases and licences of goods.
