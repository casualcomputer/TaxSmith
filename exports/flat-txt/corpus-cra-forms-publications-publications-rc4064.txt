# RC4064 Disability-Related Information - 2025

This guide is for people with impairments in physical or mental function.

## Ways to access the information

- Read the publication

    - Online format [rc4064-e.html](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/rc4064/disability-related-information.html)

    - PDF [rc4064-25e.pdf](/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pub/rc4064/rc4064-25e.pdf)

    - Large print [rc4064-lp-25e.pdf](/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pub/rc4064/rc4064-lp-25e.pdf)

- Download and listenUse your preferred screen reader to listen to the e-text file.

    - [E-text (rc4064-25e.txt)](/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pub/rc4064/rc4064-25e.txt)

- Ask for an alternate formatYou can order a digital audio, e-text, braille or large print version of this publication to be sent to you by mail.[Order alternate formats for persons with disabilities](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/help-forms-publications/about-multiple-formats.html)
